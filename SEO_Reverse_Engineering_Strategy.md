# SEO Strategy: Reverse Engineering Competitors

This strategy focuses on analyzing top-ranking competitors in the specific niche (e.g., "Dammam to Bahrain Taxi") to replicate their success.

## 1. Competitor Identification
*   **Action**: Identify 3-5 top-ranking websites for keywords like "taxi dammam to bahrain", "private car bahrain to saudi".
*   **Tools**: SEMrush, Ahrefs (or free alternatives like UberSuggest for basic data).
*   **Metric**: Look for sites with *low* Domain Rating (DR < 20) but *high* traffic. These are the "weak" competitors we can easily beat.

## 2. Reverse Engineering Structure & Keywords
*   **Site Structure Analysis**:
    *   Map out their menu hierarchy (e.g., do they have separate pages for "Khobar to Bahrain", "Dammam to Bahrain", "Airport Transfers"?).
    *   **Goal**: Create a superset of their pages. If they have 10 location pages, we create 12 better ones.
*   **Keyword Extraction**:
    *   Use tools to find which keywords drive their traffic.
    *   **Implementation**: Create dedicated pages for each high-volume keyword cluster (e.g., "Visa Run Service").

## 3. Tech Stack & Content "Theft" (Ethical)
*   **Content Gap**: Use their headings (H1, H2) as a baseline for your outlines.
    *   *Improvement*: If they write 300 words, we write 800 words of *better*, structured content (as done in the new blog post).
*   **Backlink Replication**:
    *   Analyze their backlink profile.
    *   **Action**: If they have links from local directories (e.g., Expat.com, YellowPages Bahrain), manually register your business there too.
